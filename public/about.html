Empowering through Ethereum

The Ethereum Enterprise Alliance (EEA) is a member-driven organization. Our North Star is to advance the use of Enterprise Ethereum and Mainnet Ethereum blockchain technology as an open-standard. Since its inception in 2017, the EEA has been proactive in connecting a diversity of member groups including Fortune 500 companies, startups, academics and technology vendors. The EEA has also played a significant role in promoting the adoption of blockchain technology in industries like finance, supply chain and healthcare.

Our core tenets include

Understanding enterprise requirements

Building standard specifications that addresses these requirements

Evolving alongside and include the public Ethereum blockchain

Achieving global interoperability through certification programs
